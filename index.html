2008 : Bitcoin Whitepaper

Satoshi Nakamoto publishes the Bitcoin whitepaper, introducing the concept of a decentralized digital currency.

2009 : Bitcoin Launch

The Bitcoin network goes live, and the first block (Genesis Block) is mined by Satoshi Nakamoto.

2010 : First Bitcoin Transaction

The first known purchase using Bitcoin occurs when a programmer buys two pizzas for 10,000 BTC.

2011 : Emergence of Altcoins

Other cryptocurrencies such as Litecoin and Namecoin are launched, leading to the rise of altcoins.

2013 : Bitcoin Price Surge

Bitcoin's price reaches $1,000 for the first time, drawing significant media attention.

2017 : ICO Boom

Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) become popular, raising billions for new projects and leading to regulatory scrutiny.

2020 : DeFi and NFTs

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) and Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) gain popularity, expanding the use cases for blockchain technology.

2021 : Mainstream Adoption

Major companies and institutions begin accepting cryptocurrencies, and Bitcoin reaches an all-time high of over $60,000.

2023 : Regulatory Developments

Countries around the world are implementing regulations for cryptocurrencies, shaping the future landscape of digital assets.
